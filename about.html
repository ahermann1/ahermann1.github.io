About Me

Audrey Hermann is a 2019 Eastern Washington University graduate with a B.A. in Graphic Design and a monit in Art.

I create designs that impact a community through color,symbols,and meaning. I love the art of creating a visual eperience that impacts the viewers. I find inspiration from everyday mindless activites, because the best designs should be simple and represent the world around us. Robert L. peters puts it this way, "Design creates culture. Culture shapes values. Values determine the future."
